This is Pure Bottle

Our Pure Bottle project is designed to help and nutrition the people which do not have much access to the same priviledges which we have. Using our Pure Bottle, we can help eliminate thirst and cultivate clean water sources all over the world. With our low-cost and efficient water bottle we can help reach the people which do not have as much money and give them something worth the value. Using activated carbon filtration, a heating source, and a UV light we eliminate almost all bacteria which can be found in unsafe drinking environments. Using 3d printing we are able to maintain the low-cost which we hope to offer, and keep the same or higher amount of quality compared to other brands.
